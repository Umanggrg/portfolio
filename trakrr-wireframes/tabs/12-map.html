One IA tweak I'd proposeopt

You asked me to flag if I found something compelling. Here it is — take it or leave it.

Current

Feed · Race · History · Profile

Works, but Race buries Custom Workout, Free Run, and Quick Station under a sub-screen. And History is mostly a list — it doesn't earn its own tab if the user races 2–3×/week.

→
Proposed

Feed · Train · You

Feed unchanged. Train = a vertical menu of Race / Custom / Free Run / Quick Station with the central red CTA always being "Race" — the surface athletes hit before every session. You = profile + history merged into one scrollable identity surface (hero → next up → trends → recent races). Three tabs, fewer dead-ends, history-as-a-list lives where it's already wanted.

→ For the rest of these wireframes I'm sticking with your 4-tab structure. The 3-tab version is in my back pocket if you want to explore it.
