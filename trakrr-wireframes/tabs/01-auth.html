Voice on edge states

  1. 1"taken" — one word. Not "Sorry, that handle is already in use by another user."
  2. 2"Can't reach Trakrr." — names the actor. Not "Network error."
  3. 3"We'll wait." — observational, not pleading. The app isn't anxious.
  4. 4No retry counters. No exponential-backoff messaging. The button works when you tap it.
