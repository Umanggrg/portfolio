Every string in the app, in one place. Tone is set once here so it stays consistent across feed, race, profile, push, and email. When you change a string, change it here first.

Voice principles10.1

Direct, never cute"Race ready" not "Let's gooo!" Athletes are working — respect their time. No exclamation marks except after PBs and finishes.
Numbers do the work"−42s vs PB" tells the whole story. Don't add "Amazing!" — the number is already amazing. Show, don't shout.
Coach not cheerleaderHOLD. SLOW. PUSH. We tell you what to do, not how to feel. Like the calm voice in your ear when shit's hard.
HYROX vocab is sacredStations are named correctly: "Sled Push", "Burpee Broad Jump", "Wall Balls". Never "burpees" — they're "broad jumps". Never "row machine" — it's "Row".
Empty states have edges"No races yet. Strap in." beats "You haven't recorded any workouts." Short. Active. A little sharp.
Errors blame the world"Watch lost signal" not "You forgot to enable bluetooth." Even when it's the user, we eat it.
