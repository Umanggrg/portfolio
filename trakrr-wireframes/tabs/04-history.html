Edge cases04.3

Empty (no races yet)

"No races yet. Your first finish will live here forever." + coral CTA to start a race.

Partial / DNF

Shows with grey "PARTIAL" tag. Still searchable. Excluded from PB calcs but counts toward streak.

Imported from Health

Workouts imported from Apple Health appear with a small "↓ Health" tag. Less metadata; can be augmented.

Deleted race

Hard-delete with confirm. We don't soft-delete — athletes deserve agency over their archive.

Race with no caption

List shows "Sunday HYROX sim" (auto-title from date + type). Detail invites you to add notes.

Race not yet finished

If you discarded mid-race, it doesn't appear in History at all — discard means discard.
