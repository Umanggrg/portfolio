Edge cases · the long tail02.4

You raced today

Top of feed shows your race first with a coral border + "Just now · You" tag. After 24h, it drops into the chronological stream.

Friend got first PB

PB delta in green is enough. Don't add confetti or "🎉 first PB!" — let the number speak.

Race in progress (LIVE)

If a followed athlete is mid-race, a slim LIVE banner appears at top of feed: "Sarah is racing · 4 stations in." Tap to spectate.

Deleted race

If author deletes after you reacted, the card stays but greys out with "Race no longer available." Your reaction record is cleared silently.

Blocked athlete

Their cards disappear from feed instantly, no banner. They cannot see yours either.

Comment from a follower-of-follower

Shown with a "follows X" hint under the name on first appearance. After that, just the name.

You commented, they deleted

Show "comment removed" in place. Your text is preserved in your activity log; nobody else sees it.

Empty division/nearby tab

"No races in Women's Open today. Be the first." with coral CTA to start race.
