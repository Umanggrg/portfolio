Race-flow principles03.7

One number, one verb

Elapsed time + one coaching word ("HOLD," "PUSH"). Everything else is supporting cast. No charts mid-race.

The button is huge

"Next Station" is 64pt tall, coral, glowing. You hit it with a sweaty thumb in low light. This is correct.

Pause is not destructive

Tap pause → sheet. Resume is the default. Discard takes two taps. We protect the data.

End-early is forgiving

Don't shame the user. Save partial. They can retry tomorrow.

The summary is the moment

PB delta + station grid + one specific insight ("Sled Pull, again"). Not 15 stats. This is what they came for.

Sharing is opt-in

"Save only" is equal-weight with "Post to feed." Some races are private. Respect that.
