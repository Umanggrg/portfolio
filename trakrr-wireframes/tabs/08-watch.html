Apple Watch — the differentiator

The watch is where the race lives. Phone gets the recap; the wrist gets the race. Glanceable typography, single hero metric per page, color-coded by intent. Built for arm pump, sweat, blurred vision at 180 bpm.

Hierarchy: always-on shows current segment's primary metric → glance for ghost delta → wait for an alert. Never make the athlete think mid-station.
